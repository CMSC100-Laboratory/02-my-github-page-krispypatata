Autobiography ▲

Hi, I'm Keith Ginoel S. Gabinete, born on July 30, 2001, in Lipa City, Batangas. I completed my elementary and junior high school education at Pinagkawitan Elementary School and Pinagkawitan National High School, respectively, and pursued the STEM strand for senior high school at The Mabini Academy. My favorite subject was math, which fueled my interest in problem-solving and logical thinking.

Although I had no prior knowledge of programming when I entered university, my love for math and video games ignited my curiosity about how technology and games work behind the scenes. This curiosity has motivated me to pursue a degree in Computer Science at the University of the Philippines Los Baños. As I gained more knowledge in my program, I developed a strong interest in data structures and algorithms, web programming, and game development. Additionally, I consider Java to be my favorite programming language due to its versatility and wide range of applications.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy reading manga, manhwa, or manhua. I also love watching random YouTube videos and getting a good laugh from popular memes.

Looking ahead, I’m excited to keep learning and working on new technologies. My goal is to use my skills to create interesting projects that can be useful to society.
